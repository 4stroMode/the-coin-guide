# The Coin Guide

<cy>The Coin Guide</c> is a mod that adds a guide to help you get the <cb>user coins</c> of every level. You can also find a list of <cg>free coins</c> on the search page.
This mod is <cr>under construction</c>, so expect missing features.

<cy>Features:</c>
* **Coin Guide button on levels with coins**
* **Free Coins list on the search page**
* **Admins & Moderators roles**
