# The Coin Guide

<cy>The Coin Guide</c> is a mod that adds a guide to help you get the <cb>user coins</c> of every level. You can also find a search page to find <cg>free coins</c> and more.

<cy>Features:</c>
* **Coin Guide button on levels with coins**
* **Coins Search Page**
* **Admins & Moderators roles**
* **Coin Difficulty Rating System**
